Aerospace & Defense

Our mission remains steadfast: to support the warfighter by delivering superior digital engineering solutions that ensure performance, reliability, and mission readiness through innovative, domestically executed processes.

Why Choose USL?

We recognize the critical role we play in national security and are committed to the highest standards of precision, compliance, and integrity.

Digital Engineering Expertise: Our team possesses deep technical proficiency in 3D scanning, metrology, reverse engineering, CAD modeling, and prototyping applied exclusively to hardware that demands accuracy and security.

Quality and Compliance Assurance: We maintain rigorous processes aligned with defense standards, including CMMC Level 2 and JCP certification. All work is performed domestically with 100% U.S.-manufactured outcomes where applicable, prioritizing chain-of-custody and data security.

Innovation in Digital Transformation: We invest in advanced scanning technologies and modeling methodologies to transform physical assets into technical data packages (TDPs), reducing non-recurring engineering costs, shortening lead times, and mitigating obsolescence risks.

Reliability and Responsiveness: Built on trust and proven execution, we deliver rapid, on-site or controlled-environment services that minimize program disruptions and provide dependable pathways to production.
